Places

New York

  • Manhattan
    • Food

      • Franchia - All vegan korean food. It's a bit pricy, about $45/person but well worth it. The "Duck" stone bowl bibimbap is my favorite.

      • Tiffin Wallah - Really tastey all vegetarian Indian food

      • Caracas - Arepa bar in the East Village. Sub cheese for bbq tofu!

    • Experience

      • One World Trade Observatory - I did this once with my parents when they visited town and it was incredible. I highly recommend it.

  • Brooklyn
    • Food

      • Champs Diner - Now in Bushiwich, this is basically an all vegan version of Denny's. I love the pancake slam and the cookie dough milkshake.

      • Dunwell Doughnuts - Just around the corner from Champs, Dunwell is all vegan and makes amazing doughnuts. Voted best in NY even against non-vegan options. The PB&J is incredible.
